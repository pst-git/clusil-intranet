Registration

To register for CLUSIL membership, follow the few easy steps shown here below:

click image above to enlarge

step 1  Membership

CLUSIL memberships come in 3 variants:

  • Individual (1 user) - 100€ ;
  • Organisation (up to 6 users) - 400€ ;
  • Student (1 user) ; 25€ (student proof required)

Depending on the selected type the next steps will slightly vary.

click image above to enlarge

step 2  Address

Provide the detailed address.

For Organisations, the professional address is needed for Individuals or Student the private address is requested.

click image above to enlarge

step 3  User (Head of List)

Create a user to access the CLUSIL Intranet and memberhip platform.

For Organisations, this first User will be considered as Head of List with the full rights to manage the membership (create/delete users, change membership profile, etc.)).

click image above to enlarge

step 4  Delegate

This step is only available for Organisations.

The delegate is the head-of-list's alternate for all it's roles: point of contact and membership management.

click image above to enlarge

step 5  Invovlement

CLUSIL is an association of active invovlement.

In this last step of the Registration, the working group, topic or other type of involvement is to be chosen.
